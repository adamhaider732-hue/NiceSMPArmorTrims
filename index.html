Overview

Armor that does more than protect

In vanilla Minecraft, armor trims are purely cosmetic. On Nice SMP, each trim material grants a powerful passive or reactive ability - completely server-side, so every player on vanilla Minecraft experiences them without downloading anything.

Full Set Required All four armor pieces - helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots - must be trimmed with the same material to activate. Any armor type works. Only the trim material matters.

How It Works

Three steps to power

01
Gather Materials
Collect a smithing template and enough trim material to apply it to all four armor pieces.
02
Trim Your Full Set
Visit a smithing table and apply the same trim material to your helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots. Any armor type works.
03
Equip and Play
Wear all four pieces simultaneously. Your ability activates instantly - no commands, no configuration needed.
